Podcasts

The Sports Whorl’s Viraj Patel, Ryan Dean, Yogi Patel, Justin Jones, Nate Preval, Matt Dengler, and Teerth Brahmbhatt bring you news, analysis, and the best coverage of the Premier League, MLB, NFL, and the NBA every week!
To catch all of our podcasts, please visit our Soundcloud and/or iTunes accounts. A few video podcasts are also available on our Youtube channel. Below are our three latest podcast episodes.
UEFA Champions League

May 27, 2016

Ep. 66: UEFA Champions League Final Preview w/ Viraj Patel and Ryan Dean

The Sports Whorl's Viraj Patel and Ryan Dean preview the UEFA Champions League final match that will have Real Madrid and Atletico Madrid squaring off against one another in Milan, Italy. Topics include: Real Madrid chasing their "Undecima" (1:30 mark), why Felipe Luis is thriving at Atletico (9:55), a quick discussion of the tactics and matches we’ll see in the match (19:00), and Viraj and Ryan's predictions for the final result of the match (31:55).

The second half of the podcast focuses on a bit of Premier League news, starting with the start of Jose Mourinho's reign at Manchester United (34:40), where Zlatan Ibrahimovic can end up (36:25), and Grant Xhaka’s arrival at Arsenal (42:40).

NBA

May 26, 2016

Ep. 65: NBA w/ Viraj Patel, Justin Jones, and Teerth Brahmbhatt

The Sports Whorl's Viraj Patel and Justin Jones start off this week's NBA podcast by providing their thoughts on Kyle Lowry’s sudden exit from Game 2 and the Raptors’ chances (0:15 mark), Kyrie Irving’s impact Eastern Conference finals so far (6:00), the imbalanced free agent market this summer (11:00), should the Warriors use a cap loophole to pay Stephen Curry more money next year (13:55), Justin’s thoughts on Michael Jordan’s business practices (16:00), Skip Bayless’ huge Fox Sports deal (22:00), and why the Steph Curry and the Warriors’ non-stop media coverage annoys Justin (26:15).

In the second half of the podcast, Viraj welcomes Teerth Brahmbhatt onto the show to get Teerth's impressions of the OKC-Golden State series (32:45), why OKC’s success has risen due to Kevin Durant and Russell Westbrook (39:05), Teerth’s thoughts on the Cavs/Raptors series, which the Cavs lead 3-2 (41:45), and the potential advantages of a CLE/OKC final over a CLE/GSW final in terms of travel (47:55).

TV Shows

May 26, 2016

Ep. 64: Daredevil Season Two w/ Viraj Patel and Teerth Brahmbhatt

The Sports Whorl's Viraj Patel and Teerth Brahmbhatt recap Netflix's Daredevil, Season Two. Topics include: The main antagonists in season two (1:00 mark), the single-take, biker gang fight scene in episode three (6:40), how the Daredevil writers harkened back to the comic book days in season two (10:50), what we can expect in season three next year (15:15), the role Hell’s Kitchen played in season two (19:15), various characters’ responses to Daredevil revealing his secret identity (24:50), why the “Trial of the Century” was never mentioned elsewhere in the Marvel universe (27:25), and the best actors in Daredevil season two (30:55).
